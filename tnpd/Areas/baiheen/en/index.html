About Us

Overview and Perspective

Police is the closest civil servant to the public as well as the bridge between the people and the government. In the fast-changing society, the public has great expectations for policing service. To meet the standards, police should transform from the traditional intervention governor into the service provider, introducing the spirit of entrepreneurial management and service marketing, demonstrating our outstanding service quality.
